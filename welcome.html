Study Background

Dear potential participants,

thank you for your interest in our study! This study is part of research done by the University of Munich and the Bundeswehr University about user interactions in online quiz applications. The duration of the study is 5 days and during this time you will be asked to sign in daily to our "Quizzle" online application and answer general knowledge questions. For every question you answer correctly, you will earn 20 pence. To protect your wallet (which reflects your daily earnings) and to make sure the same person is signing in everyday, we use facial recognition authentication (similar to FaceID) to sign in to your account. On the last day of the study you will be asked to answer a longer questionnaire about your experience with our Quizzle system, and for finishing that final questionnaire you will receive a bonus of GBP 2.5.

Pre-requisites:

  • You need to have access to a computer with internet connection
  • You should be able to spend 10 minutes everyday to answer the questions
  • You need to have a camera connected to your computer for the facial recognition authentication
  • You need to be able to read and write in English or German language
  • You need to be at least 18 years old to participate in this study
Steps to participate
  1. Read and accept the consent form and the data privacy statement.
  2. Fill in the registration form with your personal data.
  3. Take a selfie with your camera for the facial recognition authentication.
  4. Participate in the daily quiz and earn money.
  5. Complete the final questionnaire on the last day and earn a bonus of GBP 2.5.
